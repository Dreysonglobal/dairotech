CASE STUDY: Customer Value Management Solution

Deployed for a Leading Telecom Player in Africa

Telecommunications Africa 12-Month Engagement

Executive Summary

Dairo Tech partnered with a leading telecommunications provider in Africa to implement a comprehensive Customer Value Management (CVM) solution. The engagement focused on establishing robust CVM operations and analytics capabilities to drive customer retention, cross-selling, and upselling through data-driven insights.

The Challenge

The client faced significant challenges in customer retention with rising churn rates impacting profitability. They lacked:

  • Advanced analytics capabilities to predict customer behavior
  • Integrated campaign management systems
  • Data-driven processes for customer segmentation
  • Real-time monitoring of customer value metrics

Our Solution

We deployed a comprehensive CVM framework combining operations and analytics:

  • Established end-to-end CVM processes and governance
  • Implemented advanced machine learning models for churn prediction
  • Integrated analytics with campaign management systems
  • Developed executive dashboards for performance tracking

Solution Architecture

Data Integration Layer

Consolidated customer data from multiple sources including billing, usage, and customer service interactions

Analytics Engine

Machine learning models for churn prediction, customer segmentation, and lifetime value calculation

Campaign Management

Integrated CMS for executing targeted campaigns based on analytical insights

Performance Monitoring

Real-time dashboards tracking campaign effectiveness and customer metrics
